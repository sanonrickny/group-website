Storyboard

Customer Journey to Finding the Right Technician

Scene 1: Customer Searches for a Technician

Description: A customer, Sarah, is facing an issue with her computer. She navigates to the website, searching for an IT technician.

Scene 2: Browsing Technician Profiles

Description: Sarah lands on the homepage of the website and sees various technician profiles. She filters the options to IT Support Technicians.

Scene 3: Viewing Michael’s Profile

Description: Sarah clicks on Michael Collins' profile. She reads about his background in network administration and customer support.

Scene 4: Reading Day-to-Day Tasks

Description: Sarah reads examples of Michael’s daily tasks, such as troubleshooting and password recovery, which align with her issue.

Scene 5: Booking the Service

Description: Sarah schedules a service appointment through the website’s calendar feature.

Scene 6: Post-Service Feedback

Description: After Michael fixes Sarah’s computer, she logs back into the website to leave a 5-star review.
