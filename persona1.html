Persona 1

Persona Type: IT Support Technician

Name: Michael Collins

Age: 30

Location: Garden City, Long Island

Technical Comfort: Highly proficient with technology

Profession: IT Support Technician

"I spend most of my time troubleshooting systems for others, but I really enjoy unwinding by visiting new places that spark creativity and learning."

Back Story

  • Beginning his career as a self-taught computer enthusiast, Michael Collin, would dismantle old PC's to learn the intricacy's of the technology he grew up with. He grew up in a small town, and was always the "tech guy" for his family and friends, fixing everything, from computers to printers. After getting a degree in Computer Science in his local community college, he joined a mid-sized software company, where he honed his skills in network administration, cybersecurity, and customer support.

Frustrations

  • Michael has plenty of frustrations while working as an IT support technician, like:
  • Users coming up to him for assistance after having forgot their password, something that happens multiple times a day.
  • Dealing with stubborn customers who think restarting their computer will simply solve whatever issue they might have.
  • Dealing with outdated software that the customers or the company refuses to upgrade.

Motivations

  • Michael is passionate about helping others solve problems, as well as troubleshooting and solving problems. It's a satisfying profession where you're constantly learning, since technology is always evolving. It gives Michael a sense of accomplishment, solving these complex problems, like solving a puzzle. This keeps Michael passionate at work every day.
