Wireframe

Wireframes are a visual representation of the layout of a website. They are used to show the structure of a page and the placement of elements on the page. Wireframes are used to plan the layout of a website before it is built. They are a useful tool for designers and developers to communicate ideas and make sure everyone is on the same page.
